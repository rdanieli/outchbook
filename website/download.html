Download and install OuchBook

OuchBook is distributed directly. After purchase and license delivery, you install it like a standard macOS app and keep it in your menu bar.

See PayPal purchase flow Check compatibility first

Complete purchase

Pay through the OuchBook PayPal flow. The first release uses manual license fulfillment, so your download access and license arrive after purchase confirmation.

Receive your build and license

You receive the app package and your license by email. Keep both handy for installation and future updates.

Move the app into Applications

Drag OuchBook.app into /Applications. If macOS warns because the app is directly distributed, open it from Finder and allow it from Privacy & Security if needed.

Launch and enable

Open OuchBook, then look for OB in the menu bar. Click it, enable the app, and adjust the volume.

Install notes

  • Minimum OS: macOS 13
  • Primary target: Apple Silicon MacBooks
  • Direct-download app, not Mac App Store
  • Menu bar utility with no Dock icon

Early release reality

OuchBook is tuned around real lid-close limits on macOS. The scream is a pre-close effect, not a post-sleep audio trick.

If you slam the lid completely shut, macOS can cut the audio path before the full clip finishes.
Don’t skip the compatibility page. Hardware-specific sensor behavior can vary across MacBook models, and OuchBook is intentionally honest about what it currently supports.
